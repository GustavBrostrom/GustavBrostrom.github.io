    Backtracking
    With Sodoku

    Written without any framework, demonstrating the backtracking algorithm.

    You can click on a cell to select it. With a cell selected you can press a number (numpad or regular) to attempt to place it in the selected cell. If the number cannot be placed in the selected cell, it will not let you.

    If you click run, it will attempt to solve the board in it's current state. If it cannot solve the board, it will write it on the board.

    If you click blank, it will clear the board.

    If you click new, it will get a new, partially filled, solvable board.
